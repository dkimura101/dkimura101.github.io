My Neighborhood

Uptown

  • I've lived here since August of 2020
  • I moved here because it was mad close to my work, it's next to the train, there's an Aldi and a Target next door, and because I had friends looking for a roommate (and rent was affordable)
  • There's a load of apartment complexes around the neighborhood and you're usually passing by brick houses and black gates when you walk down the street. A lot of folk I see walking around are probably in their 20 somethings - 30 somethings. Late at night you can usually see/hear people walking around and the train rumbles with a super faint hum if it's quiet outside. Also like 15-20 minute walk from the beach where there's a bird sanctuary and a nice harbor that's always worth checking out.

My Favorite Neighborhood Sights

Montrose Beach

Mr Shawarma

Argyle

Montrose beach is where I go to decompress and be outside. I just try and find the most comfy spot and just stare out into the water.

Mr Shawarma is when I want to eat out to good food. It's kind of on the pricier side, but I always know it's going to taste good. I'm usually walking a line between deciding if I want McDonalds for the quick and dirty or Mr Shawarma for the quality.

Argyle is just one of those cool cultural pockets in Chicago. It just has a ton of good grocery stores, and a bunch of great food spots nearby.
